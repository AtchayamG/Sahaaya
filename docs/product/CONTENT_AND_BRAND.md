# Content and Brand

## Identity

- Name: Sahaaya.
- Meaning: help, support, or assistance across several Indian languages.
- Full title: Sahaaya — Family Continuity Planner.
- Tagline: When you cannot be there, your circle knows what to do.

## Voice

Warm, calm, specific, non-alarmist, and respectful of family autonomy. Prefer “review,” “rehearse,” “candidate pack,” and “trusted circle.” Avoid “guaranteed,” “protected,” “emergency coverage,” “AI guardian,” and “fully prepared.”

## Core pitch

Sahaaya turns scattered household knowledge into a cited continuity map, rehearses a temporary absence, and produces verified, human-approved action packs for trusted people.

## Required disclosures

- Demo uses fictional simulated data.
- Sahaaya is a planning aid, not emergency, medical, legal, financial, or safeguarding advice.
- No external messages or actions are sent.
