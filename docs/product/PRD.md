# Product Requirements Document

## Problem

Households often rely on one person’s memory for routines, contacts, and fallback decisions. When that person is unexpectedly unavailable, trusted people may have good intentions but incomplete, contradictory, or unsafe instructions.

## User promise

In under five minutes, rehearse a 72-hour absence, see what would break, assign trusted owners, and export reviewed action packs.

## Primary user

An adult household organizer coordinating a multigenerational family or trusted support circle.

## P0 requirements

- Fictional five-responsibility Replay scenario.
- Bounded text capture and optional real voice transcription.
- Cited responsibility map, gap list, and three questions.
- Scenario simulation with at least one visible dependency conflict.
- Approve/revise/reject decision for every assignment.
- Fail-closed candidate compilation and separate verification.
- Role-pack and receipt export.
- Explicit privacy and non-advice boundaries.
- Responsive, accessible, credential-free public demo.

## Non-goals

No accounts, sync, persistence, messaging, calendar writes, emergency dispatch, identity vault, medical record, financial transaction, legal document, or production household automation.

## Success metrics

- Golden path completes in under 150 seconds in the demo.
- All five responsibilities are visible and cited.
- Rejection visibly blocks compilation.
- Verification reports exact deterministic assertions.
- A first-time judge can understand the problem and outcome without setup.
