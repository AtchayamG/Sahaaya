# Sahaaya — Family Continuity Planner

## Tagline

When you cannot be there, your circle knows what to do.

## Category

## Short description

Sahaaya captures essential household knowledge, finds missing dependencies, rehearses a temporary absence, and creates cited, human-approved action packs for trusted family and caregivers.

## Story outline

- Inspiration: households often depend on one person’s undocumented memory.
- What it does: maps five responsibilities, exposes gaps, rehearses a 72-hour absence, requires assignment approval, and verifies role packs.
- How it works: GPT-5.6 proposes grounded structured analysis; deterministic TypeScript validates, gates, compiles, and verifies.
- Safety: fictional public demo, no secrets/persistence/messages/advice, manual sharing only.
- Challenges: grounding, consent, conflict detection, and separating candidate packs from verified packs.
- What is next: opt-in encrypted workspaces and preview-first integrations after explicit consent.
