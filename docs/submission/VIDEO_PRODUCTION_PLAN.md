# Video Production Plan

## Deliverables

- Public YouTube video, maximum three minutes.
- Professional 16:9 thumbnail.
- English timed captions reviewed against the final narration.
- Final MP4, SRT, thumbnail, and upload copy stored under `docs/submission/`.

## Production sequence

1. Freeze the verified public build and run the golden path.
2. Capture the application at 1920x1080 without secrets or personal data.
3. Generate narration from the approved script with a calm, warm, globally intelligible premium voice available on this system.
4. Mix narration for clear speech; avoid music that competes with explanations.
5. Render under three minutes and visually inspect the opening, all state transitions, and final frame.
6. Generate and proofread SRT captions, then upload them in YouTube Studio even if automatic captions are available.
7. Upload the thumbnail, title, description, and public visibility; verify the public URL in a signed-out context.

## Truthfulness gate

The video may show only behavior reproduced from the final public commit. Live GPT-5.6 output must be identified as live; deterministic Replay output must remain visibly simulated.
